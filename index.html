Hi, I'm Ehsan Zibaei!

I'm originally from the city of Tabriz, Iran, where my passion for engineering began. I pursued this passion by earning my BSc and MSc degrees in Aerospace Engineering from Sharif University of Technology and University of Tehran.

In 2017, I embarked on an exciting journey to Munich, Germany, joining the Chair of Software and Systems Engineering at the Technical University of Munich as a research and teaching assistant, a role I enjoyed until 2023. Afterwards, from 2023 to 2024, I continued my adventure in Munich as an R&D software engineer at Elektrobit Automotive GmbH.

My professional enthusiasm centers around robotics and AI, particularly focusing on motion planning and control solutions. In the Projects section, you can explore some of my endeavors, mostly regarding quadrotor aerial robots.
