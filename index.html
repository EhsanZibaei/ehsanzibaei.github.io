Hi, I'm Ehsan Zibaei!

I'm originally from the city of Tabriz, Iran, where my passion for engineering began. I pursued this passion by earning my BSc and MSc degrees in Aerospace Engineering from Sharif University of Technology (2013) and University of Tehran (2015).

From 2017 to 2023, I worked as a full-time researcher at the Chair of Software and Systems Engineering at the Technical University of Munich. Afterwards, from 2023 to 2024, I worked as a Control Systems Engineer at Elektrobit Automotive GmbH, a subsidiary of Continental AG.

Recently I started working as a freelance software engineer. In this role, I work with various R&D teams, supporting them in the design and implementation of control and sensor-fusion solutions for automotive, robotics and aerospace applications. In the age of AI, my particular strength lies in finding the right balance between cutting-edge technology and what is actually feasible and robust in real-world products. This ability is grounded in many years of hands-on experience in both academic research and industrial development environments.

In the Projects section, you can explore some of my past experience related to autonomous systems.

If you are developing a new product or considering the integration of new sensor or AI technologies and are unsure about technical feasibility, architecture choices or integration risks, I would be happy to discuss your project with you and support you from the initial concept through to implementation.
